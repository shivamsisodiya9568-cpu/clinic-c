Health Tips

Small habits that protect long-term health.

Hydration

Drink water steadily through the day.

Agra summers can be intense. Keep hydration consistent and seek care for dizziness or severe weakness.

Prevention

Track blood pressure and sugar.

Regular checks help detect changes early, especially for adults with family history or lifestyle risk.

Follow-up
